Common Questions

What is the success rate for women over 35?

In 2026, using AI-selection and genomic stimulation, Motherhood IVF has achieved a 68% success rate for patients aged 35-39, a significant increase over the last decade.

How does the AI select the best embryo?

The AI evaluates 2,400+ morphokinetic data points including the exact timing of cell division and metabolic consumption, which are invisible to the human eye.

Is the treatment covered by insurance?

Many modern health plans now recognize IVF as an essential service. We provide a dedicated financial counselor to help you navigate your specific coverage.

Can I choose the gender of the embryo?

Gender selection is governed by local medical regulations. We prioritize medical screening (PGT-M) to prevent hereditary genetic disorders.

How many clinic visits are required?

A standard cycle requires 5 to 7 monitoring visits. Many of our initial consultations can now be performed via our secure Tele-Health portal.
